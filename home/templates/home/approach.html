Our Approach

Our Third Party and Vendor Risk Framework™ is our proprietary tool for helping organisations to establish the right overall approach to Third Party and Vendor Risk management.

find out more

Govenance

Monitoring and reviewing the framework to ensure that it is designed and operating effectively

Establishing clear accountabilities for who does what across the Third Party and Vendor life cycle

Policies, Standards & Procedures

Establishing a clear set of requirements and activities that must be performed to effectively manage Third Party and Vendor risks

Ensuring policies and standards meet all existing legal and regulatory requirements

Ensuring that people and teams impacted by policies, standards and procedures are suitably aware, trained and have the right level of support

3rd Party & Vendor Inventory

Establishing a clear definition for Third Party and Vendors

Producing and maintaining a complete and accurate inventory of Third Party and Vendor relationships and services

Risk Profiling & Segmentation

Establishing a consistent way to risk assess the Third Party and Vendor base to differentiate the risk profile and to establish an appropriate and proportional management response
